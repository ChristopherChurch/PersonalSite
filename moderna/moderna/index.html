About Me

My name is Christopher James Rodriguez. I am a visual designer. I earned a BA in Art+Design and minored in Marketing Communications with the idea in mind that Design and Marketing should be integrated completely. With the rise of UX, I discovered a new door that opened up a world of revelations and epiphanies about the beliefs I had throughout my interdisciplinary studies in college. I believe that needs should dictate design. I have a drive to keep growing and learning, and I am looking to work with others that can enable that quality in me, and moreover, with people whom I can ignite that same feeling within.
